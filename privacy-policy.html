BlockPuzzle - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Introduction

BlockPuzzle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

BlockPuzzle does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game works entirely offline and does not require any user data to function.

Data Storage

Game progress (such as completed levels) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics (via Firebase Analytics) to understand general usage patterns such as which levels are played most frequently. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics - for anonymous usage statistics

Children's Privacy

BlockPuzzle does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The game is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
